# Hackathon Pitch

## Problem

Fixed timers waste green time. One road may be empty while another has a growing queue. This increases waiting time, fuel burn, and emissions.

## Solution

Traffic Optimizer Pro turns a junction into an adaptive system. It detects lane demand, scores competing phases, and allocates green time dynamically.

## Demo story

- Baseline signal uses a fixed cycle.
- Adaptive signal gives more green to the overloaded corridor.
- Dashboard proves the impact using wait time, queue, throughput, and emissions metrics.
- The video analysis panel shows how live CCTV can estimate lane density.

## Impact

- lower wait time
- lower congestion
- faster emergency clearance
- lower idle emissions
- better green-time utilization

## Why it stands out

- fully working stack instead of just slides
- simulation plus frontend plus API plus CV demo
- designed for Indian mixed-traffic constraints
- easy to extend into smart-city deployments
